GitarTheory

Trying to understand guitar chords

Fueled by my desire to kill time and my love for music, I began GitarTheory in 2017. GitarTheory is a very basic guitar chord database that aims to teach chord construction.

The program is very rugged, but it receives updates here and there with a more extensive and polished database in mind as the end goal.
